What is the difference in eggs from caged hens and cage-free hens?

Cage-free eggs have more advantages than caged eggs. Because cage-free eggs are allowed to roam free and have access to outside spaces where they can graze on grass. This makes cage-free eggs twice more nutritious than caged eggs. Cage-free hens are happy hens which produce happy eggs.
